K & K CAFE

Welcome to K&K Café, your cozy and inviting retreat nestled in the scenic Ambadare Ranges in Kinangop, Kenya. Since opening our doors in 2005, we have been dedicated to serving the community with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and a passion for great food. Our café offers a warm, friendly ambiance, perfect for catching up with friends over breakfast, enjoying a leisurely lunch, or sipping a cup of our signature artisanal coffee. Whether youre craving classic comfort food or exploring healthier, plant-based options, K&K Café has something for everyone. Come in, relax, and let us brighten your day!
